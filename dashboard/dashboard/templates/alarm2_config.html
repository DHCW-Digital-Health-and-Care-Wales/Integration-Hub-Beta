How Alarm 2 works
The alarm queries customMetrics in Log Analytics for the messages_sent metric, filtered by Health Board and Peer Service dimensions over the configured Window. It fires when the total count of messages sent is at or below the Threshold. Once fired, it will not re-fire until the Alerting Gap has elapsed.
